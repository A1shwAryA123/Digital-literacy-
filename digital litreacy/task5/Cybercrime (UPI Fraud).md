# Cybercrime (UPI Fraud)



One situation where poor digital communication can cause problems is when a student sends an email to a professor without a proper subject line or respectful tone. For example, writing a message like “Send me notes” without any greeting or context may appear rude and unprofessional. This can create a negative impression and may lead to the request being ignored. Proper communication should include a clear subject, polite greeting, and structured message explaining the request. Using respectful language and maintaining a formal tone can improve understanding and increase the chances of receiving a positive response. This highlights the importance of following proper email etiquette in academic and professional environments.
