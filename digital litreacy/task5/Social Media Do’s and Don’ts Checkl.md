# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts Checklist



## &#x20;Do’s

Maintain a professional and respectful tone in your posts

Think before posting any content

Use privacy settings to control your information

Share useful and positive content

Build a strong and clean digital profile



## &#x20;Don’ts

Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly

Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content

Do not engage in online arguments or negativity

Avoid clicking on suspicious links

Do not spread misinformation or fake news
