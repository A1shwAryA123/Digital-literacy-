Prevention Checklist (8+ Points):~

Stay Safe Online – Prevention Tips:

1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone...
2. Do not approve unknown “collect requests” on UPI apps...
3. Always verify the identity of the person requesting money...
4. Avoid clicking on suspicious links received via SMS or email...
5. Use strong passwords and enable app security features...
6. Regularly check your bank statements and transaction history...
7. Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store or App Store...
8. Do not respond to calls claiming to be from banks asking for details...
9. For financial safety, double-check payment details before sending money...
10. Immediately report fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call 1930...
